Password Safety Awareness

Passwords are the first line of defense online. Yet, survey data shows 40% of young adults rarely update their passwords or reuse the same one across accounts.

Watch: Password Safety Tips üîê

Common Mistakes

  • Using weak passwords like 123456 or password123.
  • Reusing the same password on multiple accounts.
  • Not enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

Best Practices

  • Create strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols).
  • Use a password manager instead of writing passwords down.
  • Change passwords regularly, especially after a data breach.
  • Enable 2FA for email, banking, and social accounts.

Emerging Trends

  • Passwordless login (biometric, OTP-based systems).
  • Credential stuffing attacks (using leaked passwords from other sites).
