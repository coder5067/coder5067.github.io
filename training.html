Cybersecurity Training Modules

The training program consists of 4 Interactive Modules that cover key topics such as Phishing, Safe Browsing, Password Safety, and Social Engineering.

These modules are designed to raise awareness about common cybersecurity risks, sharpen your ability to identify threats, and promote safer online behavior. The study is aimed at Young Adults (23–39 years Old).

Modules Covered:

Phishing

Learn how cybercriminals trick people with fake emails, SMS, or websites that look real.

Safe Browsing
