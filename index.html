Why We Created This Website

With the increase in cases of cybercriminal activities including phishing, malware attacks, and data breaches among others, university students are at high risk despite they rarely possess adequate information to protect themselves. This is especially important because as the world goes online, social engineering in terms of acceptable behaviours needs to be well facilitated through structured education.

In the current hyperconnected world, the age group of young adults is one of the most digitally active populations. They explore a wide range of work, communication, shopping, financial, and entertainment websites. This has heightened their vulnerability to advanced online threats, including phishing, internet frauds, identity theft, and information breaches.

Most young adults feel comfortable in the world of technology but many are unaware of scammers and how they can be persuaded through urgency, emotion, and trust. With such threats, high-quality targeted cybersecurity awareness programs are urgently required that are specific to the actions, tendencies, and online activities of this extreme user base group.
